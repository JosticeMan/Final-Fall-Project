What do you use to catch Pokemon?

In the pokemon world, you use items called Pokeballs to catch Pokemon in the game. Pokeballs have many variants, some of which are better than the others.

Pokeball Type Description
PokeBall: These are regular Pokeballs and can be purchased through the Pokemon Go Shop.
Great Ball: Great Pokeballs cannot be purchased through shop. These Poke Balls have higher chance to capture Pokemon then regular ones.
Ultra Ball: Ultra Pokeballs are the best in Pokemon Go. Ultra Balls have the highest capture rate out of all the available balls.
Master Ball: Master Pokeball is Unavailable in Pokemon Go. Players, however, were able to decode the games data to find that it does indeed exist within the game.

Based on the Pokemon Go Beta information this ball had a 100% Capture and Hit Rate even if you missed.

Tips for Catching Pokemon

Land The Pokeball Inside the circle ring that shrinks. Landing a Nice throw or better will increase your capture rate.

If you want the highest chance of catching the Strongest Pokemon In Pokemon Go, you need to wait until the ring inside the circle is smaller. That’s the advice straight from the developers.

When you start seeing orange and red rings around pokemon, you’ll maximize your chances of success as much as you can — This is an especially useful once you start trying to capture The stronger Pokémon with Great and Master Balls.
